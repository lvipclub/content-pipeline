**Post Text:**
The intersection of IAQ and energy efficiency is no longer a trade-off — it's a code requirement.

Singapore's BCA Green Mark 2026 now mandates CO₂-based DCV in all new office builds. ASHRAE 90.1-2025 follows suit.

Demand-controlled ventilation that responds to real-time occupancy saves 30-40% on outdoor air treatment costs while maintaining CO₂ below 800 ppm [ASHRAE 62.1-2022]. The old model of fixed outdoor air fraction is dead.

Specifiers: insist on BACnet/Modbus breathing-zone sensors. Proprietary protocols won't carry the submetering data your energy audit needs at 15-min granularity [ISO 50002].
